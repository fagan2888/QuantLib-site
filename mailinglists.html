Mailing Lists

Choose a list to browse, search, and post messages.
  • quantlib-users:
    QuantLib users general discussion list. This list is for everything related to the QuantLib usage and development. Topics about quantitative finance in general are also accepted.
    (go to Archives, Subscribe/Unsubscribe/Preferences)
  • quantlib-announce:
    QuantLib announcement list (very low traffic). You cannot post to this list. If you have an announcement for the QuantLib community, please post it on quantlib-users: the QuantLib admin will forward it to quantlib-announce if appropriate.
    (go to Archives, Subscribe/Unsubscribe/Preferences)
  • quantlib-jobs:
    QuantLib jobs list. This list is for supply and demand of quantitative finance jobs. You can post here job offers, links to job offers, resumes. HTML and PDF are preferred. Attachment are allowed as long as they are not too big. All messages are archived for later browsing: please state how long your message is valid. No multiple post, please.
    (go to Archives, Subscribe/Unsubscribe/Preferences)
  • quantlib-cvs:
    QuantLib CVS checkin messages (very high traffic). Every time the QuantLib CVS code changes an email documenting the change is automatically generated. Suggested for QuantLib developers only.
    (go to Archives, Subscribe/Unsubscribe/Preferences)
